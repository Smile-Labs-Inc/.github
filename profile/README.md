# Smile Labs

We're a small engineering team from Sri Lanka. We build AI-driven products and
enter a lot of hackathons — usually starting from a real problem (health records
stuck on paper, farmers with no market data, news that's more heat than facts)
and turning it into something people can actually use.

**[smilelabs.online](https://smilelabs.online/)**

## Products

| Project | What it is |
| --- | --- |
| [sentilex-ai-advocate](https://github.com/Smile-Labs-Inc/sentilex-ai-advocate) | An autonomous AI legal advocate for trauma victims in Sri Lanka. |
| [SmartBabyLK-X2](https://github.com/Smile-Labs-Inc/SmartBabyLK-X2) | A digital health companion that turns paper infant-health records into AI-assisted care. |
| [growa-core-engine](https://github.com/Smile-Labs-Inc/growa-core-engine) | Crop guidance, market intelligence, and pest detection built for Sri Lankan smallholder farms. |
| [fluxcard-firmware](https://github.com/Smile-Labs-Inc/fluxcard-firmware) | Firmware for FluxCard — a battery-less, NFC-powered point-of-care diagnostic device. |
| [Persona](https://github.com/Smile-Labs-Inc/Persona) | AI personas for faster, cheaper market research. |
| [iwb25-192-sharks](https://github.com/Smile-Labs-Inc/iwb25-192-sharks) | Rewrites sensationalized news into neutral, factual reporting using NLP. |
